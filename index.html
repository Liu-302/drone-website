Project Overview

The Drone Explorer: Immersive Visualization for UAV Mission Planning

SkyPath enables users to analyze, simulate, and refine UAV flight paths and viewpoints interactively in a 3D environment. It bridges the gap between algorithmic path generation and human-guided mission design, ensuring efficient, adaptive, and visually optimized aerial operations.
